Opencart-Odoo-Bridge(OOB)

Bi-Directional Export/Update of data between Opencart and Opencart-Odoo-Bridge

If you want your Opencart e-commerce store to synchronize with Odoo (formerly known as openERP) and also from Odoo to Opencart, then Opencart Odoo Bridge (OOB) is the product suitable for your requirement.

Opencart-Odoo-Bridge(OOB) module allows you to Export/Update products, product's categories, product attributes and attribute values to Opencart. Orders are also imported in real-time from Opencart to Odoo.

Easy Configuration of Module

Just fill in the Base URL of your Opencart Instance

Then fill in the Api Key generated at Opencart.

Fill in other other details according to your choice and then test the connection.

One Click Product Export/Update to Opencart

Export all the new Products at once or export selected products

Export all Products to Opencart

Export all the new products (which are not already exported to Opencart), with just one button click.

Export a single Product

You can select and export only one or seleted products to Opencart

Stay informed about all operations with success/failure messages on each operation.
